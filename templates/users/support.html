📘 How to Create an Invoice

1

Go to Invoices

Tap the "Invoices" tab and click the "Create New" button at the top right.

2

Select Client & Details

Choose an existing client or add a new one. Fill in items, quantities, and prices.

3

Review & Send

Preview the invoice, add notes, then click "Send Email" or "Download PDF".
