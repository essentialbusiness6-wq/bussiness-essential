Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026. We respect your data and are transparent about how we collect, use, and protect it.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to provide and improve our services:

  • Account Data: Name, email, company name, and billing information.
  • Usage Data: Login timestamps, feature usage metrics, and error logs (anonymized).
  • Client/Invoice Data: Data you upload or create within the platform. We process it solely to deliver invoices and reports.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively for:

  • Processing payments, generating invoices, and managing subscriptions.
  • Sending transactional emails, security alerts, and product updates (opt-out available).
  • Improving platform performance, detecting fraud, and complying with legal obligations.

We never sell, rent, or share your business data with advertisers or data brokers.

3. Data Security & Retention

Security is foundational to our architecture:

  • All data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256).
  • We perform regular third-party penetration tests and SOC 2 Type II audits.
  • Inactive accounts are securely deleted after 36 months of inactivity, per our retention policy. You may request immediate deletion at any time.

4. Third-Party Services

We integrate with trusted providers to enable core functionality:

  • Stripe/PayPal: Payment processing. They operate under strict PCI-DSS compliance.
  • SendGrid: Transactional email delivery.
  • AWS: Cloud hosting and encrypted backups.

Each partner adheres to GDPR/CCPA standards. We maintain Data Processing Agreements (DPAs) with all processors.

5. Your Rights (GDPR/CCPA)

Depending on your jurisdiction, you have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or export your personal data.
  • Request deletion of your account and all associated data.
  • Opt-out of marketing communications at any time.
  • Lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

To exercise these rights, email privacy@businesse.com or use the in-app "Data Export" and "Delete Account" tools.
